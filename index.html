MY STORY

Hello,
I am Pavel Voloshin

▼

I'm an artist and a sculptor, living in Minsk.

I create pictures, statuettes, panels but mostly devote myself to bronze sculpture.

I like to use both classical and modern instruments, mixing modeling, drawing, casting, 3D modeling, digital graphics, processing on software-controlled machines, laser and waterjet cutting, welding, bending, etc.

After graduating from the Minsk Polytechnic College, I explored various opportunities. In 2010 I became a part of a workshop team specializing in the technical implementation of artistic ideas, and started working on my first projects.

Since 2017 I have been working independently, expanding directions and experimenting with techniques and technologies.
